DANIEL SMITH

After getting my PhD from Penn State University, I worked in academia for over a decade, serving as a faculty-member at the University of South Carolna and, later, the University of Texas at Austin. I have left academia behind to pursue a new career as a full-stack web developer.

Apart from hanging out with my young son, Joshua, I spend most of my time immersed in the world of technology and coding. I am profoundly curious about the relationship between languages and communication (both human and computer) and how that dynamic relationship shapes the communities in which we work, play, and live our lives.
